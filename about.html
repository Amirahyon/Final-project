A Digital Library to Empower You Against Gender-based Violence

Vision

Safehaven Tech Team aims to eradicate gender-based violence, promoting access to information and resources for everyone, ensuring a free and safe environment.

Mission

Safehaven Tech Team aims to empower individuals and communities by providing evidence-based, culturally appropriate, and easy-to-use digital resources to end gender-based violence.

How We Work

Our Objectives on how we operates

User-friendly

Create a comprehensive, user-friendly digital library of resources that supports people affected by gender-based violence.

Information Resource

Provide information and resources that are accessible to everyone, regardless of their location, language, or socioeconomic status.

Empowerment

Empower individuals to take action to prevent and address gender-based violence, and to create a safer and more just world for everyone.
